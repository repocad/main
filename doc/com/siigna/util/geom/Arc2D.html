com.siigna.util.geom

Arc2D

case class Arc2D (center: Vector2D, radius: Double, startAngle: Double, angle: Double) extends Arc with GeometryBasic2D with Product with Serializable

A mathematical representation of a 2-dimensional arc i. e. a circle piece. An arc is drawn counter-clockwise, so positive values draws an arc counter-clockwise, and negative values draws it clockwise.

center

The center of the circle representing the arc.

radius

The radius of the circle representing the arc.

startAngle

The starting angle of the arc. In degrees between 0 and 360.

angle

The number of degrees the arc is spanning.
