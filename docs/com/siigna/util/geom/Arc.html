com.siigna.util.geom

Arc

case class Arc (start: Vector, middle: Vector, end: Vector) extends BasicGeometry with Product with Serializable

A mathematical representation of an arc from three vectors, representing any three points on the circumference of a circle - start, middle and end.

Important: Assumes that (start != middle != end).
