Ellipse

case class Ellipse (f1: Vector, f2: Vector, p: Vector) extends Geometry with Product with Serializable

Utilities for an ellipse The ellipse is represented in the form (x/a)^2 + (y/b)^2 = 1.

Pre-condition: p.x != a. If the pre-condition isn't fulfilled, the ellipse is created with a very small b.
